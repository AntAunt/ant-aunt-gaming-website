Beatrix is an Explorer participating in the Tereasure Race. She uses wind magic and wields a standard issue Empire Knight Wind Commander Spear as her weapon. Beatrix used to be a trainee for the Empress' bodyguard unit, as her mother did previously, gathering some reputation as the face for the Empire's soldiers. After her mother's passing, she quit and started operating solo. Despite this, people still associate Beatrix the Empire. She joined the Treasure Race to prove that she does not need the Empire to succeed on her own.
