Beatrix Mercedes Mach V is an independent Explorer operating around Imperial Capital. She commands wind magic and uses a standard issue Empire Knight Wind Commander Spear as her weapon. Beatrix used to be a trainee for being the Empress' bodyguard, as her predcessors did previously, but due to conflicts had aggressively quit the postition. She now runs errands for villages and towns around the capital, like a hero of sorts. She joined the Treasure Hunt to prove that she is capable on her own without the Empire's support.
